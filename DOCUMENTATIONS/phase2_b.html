Web cookies (also called HTTP cookies or browser cookies) are small pieces of data that a website stores on your computer (inside your browser). They help websites remember information about you — like your login, preferences, or what’s in your shopping cart — so that you don’t have to re-enter it every time. A cookie is usually stored as a key–value pair. Cookie-parser is a tool that helps Express apps read and manage cookies easily
